Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Description

This recipe will make the most delicious pulled pork sandwhiches with minimal effort!

Ingredients

  • 1 (2pound) pork tenderloin
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle of root beer
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle of your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 8 hamburger buns, split and lightly toasted

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Place pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour root beer over top
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 7 hours
  4. Drain slow cooker and stir in barbecue sauce
  5. Server and hamburger buns and enjoy
