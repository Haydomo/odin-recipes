Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Description

First gather your ingreidents (see ingredients below). Second place your pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour root beer over top.

Cover and cook on low for 6 to 7 hours. Drain the slow cooker and stir in your barbecue sauce. Serve and hamburger buns and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 (2pound) pork tenderloin
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle of root beer
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle of your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 8 hamburger buns, split and lightly toasted
